INSTRUCTIONS

We are researchers at the University of Texas at Austin studying content moderation with the goal of making the Internet a better place. To experiment with each unblurring feature, click any of the following buttons (default is click):

  • click: Click regions of the image to unblur it.
  • mouse over: Mouse over (use the cursor) to temporarily unblur regions.
  • slider: Use the slider below the image to gradually increase/decrease the blur.
